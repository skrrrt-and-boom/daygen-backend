## FAQ

**Q: What happens to my subscription credits if I don't use them all?**
> They reset to zero at the end of your billing cycle. Unused subscription credits do not roll over.

**Q: Will my top-up credits ever expire?**
> No! Top-up credits are permanent and never expire. Use them whenever you want.

**Q: Which credits are used first?**
> Subscription credits are always used first. Top-up credits are only touched after your subscription allowance is exhausted.

**Q: What is the "grace" buffer?**
> A small ~50 credit buffer that lets you complete a job even if you're slightly over zero. This prevents jobs from failing mid-generation.

**Q: Can I have a subscription AND buy top-ups?**
> Yes! Many users combine a subscription for regular use with occasional top-ups for busy periods.

**Q: What happens if I cancel my subscription?**
> Your subscription credits will be available until the end of your current billing period. Your top-up credits remain available forever.
